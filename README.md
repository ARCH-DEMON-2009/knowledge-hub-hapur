# Janhitkari Library

**Empowering Knowledge for Everyone**

A completely free and disciplined study library in Ramgarhi, Hapur, Uttar Pradesh, India. Dedicated to providing a peaceful, fully-equipped study environment for every student.

## 🌟 About Janhitkari Library

Janhitkari Library is a community-driven initiative founded by **Bijander Kumar** to provide free access to quality study spaces and resources. Located in Ramgarhi, Hapur, UP, the library is dedicated to supporting students in their academic journey without any financial burden.

## 🎯 Library Rules & Conduct

To maintain a disciplined and peaceful environment, the library has established clear rules:

1. **Maintain Silence** - Keep noise levels minimal for others' concentration
2. **Proper Attire** - Dress appropriately for a study space
3. **No Mobile Phones** - Use silent mode; avoid calls and loud notifications
4. **Respect Property** - Do not damage books or equipment
5. **Organize Your Space** - Keep your study area clean and organized
6. **Follow Schedule** - Adhere to library timings and rules
7. **ID/Registration** - Carry valid ID for entry verification
8. **Return Materials** - Return borrowed items on time

## 💡 Common Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is the library really free?**
A: Yes! Janhitkari Library is completely free for all students. There are no hidden charges or membership fees.

**Q: What are the timings?**
A: We are open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, 7 days a week.

**Q: Do I need an ID to enter?**
A: Yes, bring a valid ID (student ID, Aadhar, etc.) for entry verification.

**Q: Can I use Wi-Fi without registration?**
A: Registration takes just a few minutes, then you can enjoy our free Wi-Fi.

**Q: Are there any age restrictions?**
A: No, students of all ages are welcome. We serve school children to professional exam aspirants.

**Q: Can I bring food and drinks?**
A: Water and light refreshments are allowed, but meal eating should be avoided to keep the library clean.

For more questions, visit our [FAQ page](https://janhitkari-library-hapur.vercel.app/faq).
